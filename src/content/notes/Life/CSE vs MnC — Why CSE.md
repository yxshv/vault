# CSE over MnC — My Reasons

> Written during JoSAA 2026 deliberations. Based on my actual goals, not what sounds impressive.

---

## Curriculum

- CSE has OS, Networks, Compilers, Databases — the mental models for *how things work underneath*. Understanding how a browser compositor works, why certain animations are jank-free, how GPUs pipeline render calls — all of this lives in CSE.
- MnC replaces those with Real Analysis, Abstract Algebra, Number Theory, Optimization. Genuinely beautiful math, but not load-bearing for design engineering or frontend craft.
- MnC has no dedicated systems courses. I'd be missing the exact layer that makes a design engineer technically deep, not just visually good.

## Career Path (Design Engineering)

- My target companies — Apple, Linear, Vercel, Figma, Notion — do not do campus placements in India. The path there is portfolio → public presence → direct application. Branch name is irrelevant to them.
- Campus placements don't matter for my field. Anyone arguing placement stats is thinking about a different career than mine.
- Brand matters barely at all internationally. My Twitter, GitHub, and work get me the first conversation faster than any college name.

## Earning in College

- Freelance frontend/design engineering work is the fastest path to money in year 1.
- MnC's heavier abstract math load would genuinely eat into time I'd use for freelance projects and building in public.
- CSE gives me more time margin to actually ship things.

## Peer Ecosystem

- CSE cohorts have the builders, hackathon people, open source contributors — my people.
- MnC cohorts skew toward competitive programmers, quant aspirants, researchers. Different goals.
- The people I build with in years 1–4 shape what feels normal and ambitious to me.

## The One Exception

- **IIT Bombay or IIT Delhi MnC** — if that were the choice, take it. Brand + flexibility there makes the curriculum gap walkable.
- **IIT Kharagpur or IIT Roorkee MnC** — worth serious consideration over a newer IIT CSE, given the old IIT brand and alumni network depth.
- Everywhere else: CSE wins clearly for my path.

## The Real Reason

I've been deferring what I actually want to do for 2 years because of JEE. Design engineering is not a backup — it's the thing. CSE is the branch that lets me start betting on that immediately, without fighting the curriculum to get to what I love.
